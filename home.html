About TIM

At first IT-PLN named STT-PLN operated since 1998, with the location of the campus at that time in the Indonesia Power Building, Jl. Gatot Subroto. The IT-PLN manager is the PLN Education and Welfare Foundation, abbreviated as YPK-PLN. YPK-PLN was established by PLN in the same year, 1998. In January 2020 STT-PLN changed its form to IT-PLN which consists of 4 (four) Faculties: - Faculty of Electricity and Renewable Energy - Faculty of Energy Technology and Business - Faculty of Infrastructure and Regional Technology - Faculty of Energy Telematics The main purpose of establishing IT-PLN is to form a forum for PLN experts to transfer their knowledge to the next generation. Along with the increasing demand for electrical energy, large-scale power plants using fuel oil, coal and gas have also begun to be built. Similarly, Geothermal Power Plant (PLTP). The very rapid growth of demand in Java has led to the birth of the Java-Bali interconnection system with a length of up to thousands of kilometers and using an extra high voltage of 500 kV. With PLN's background as above, skilled personnel from the disciplines of Civil, Electrical, Mechanical and Informatics engineering as long as PLN exists, become a necessity. For this reason, IT-PLN was established.
